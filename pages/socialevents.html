    • Social Events

    The Social Event will be held at The Night Safari (http://www.nightsafari.com.sg) on July 8, 2015 between 6.30 p.m to 10.15 pm. The Buses will depart from the Marina Bay Sands Convention Center at 5.15 pm and the Last Bus will leave from The Night Safari for the Marina Bay Sands Hotel at 10.15 pm.


    The Night Safari is a 12-time winner (of Best Attraction category) awarded by Singapore Tourism Board. Being the world’s first nocturnal zoo, it has attracted more than 1.1 million visitors every year.


    Constructed at a cost of S$63 million, it currently houses over 2,500 animals representing over 130 species, of which 38% are threatened species.


    The Night Safari is divided into seven geographical zones, which can be explored either by a guided tram or on foot via several walking trails (e.g., Leopard Trail, Wallaby Trail). The animals are made visible by lighting that resembles moonlight, even though it is slightly brighter than full moonlight by a few orders of magnitude. However, it is dim enough so as not to disturb the nocturnal and crepuscular animals’ behaviour.


    Also, do not miss the amazing cultural performances with tribal dances and fire eating displays (e.g., Thumbuakar Performance) and the must-see Creatures of the Night Show!


    So, do join us at the social event of PACIS 2015 for a memorable night. The Social Event ticket includes:

    • Park admission ticket
    • Tram service
    • International Buffet, with special Singaporean flavors and vegetarian items included
    • Beverage
    • Beer
    • Thumbuakar Performance Show: 6.45pm, 8.00pm, 9.00pm and 10.00pm
    • Creatures of the Night Show: 7.30pm, 8.30pm, 9.30pm and 10.30pm
